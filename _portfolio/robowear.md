## Project Overview

RoboWear is a research project focused on enabling robots to assist with dressing tasks, specifically putting on T-shirts. This challenging manipulation task requires handling deformable objects (cloth) and ensuring safe, force-limited interactions with humans.

**Status**: Under Review at RSS 2026

**Lab**: [Cornell EmPRISE Lab](https://emprise.cs.cornell.edu/)

- **Cloth Manipulation**: Learning-based approaches for handling deformable objects

- **Long-horizon Planning**: Breaking down complex dressing tasks into manageable sub-tasks

## Impact

This work contributes to assistive robotics, particularly for individuals with limited mobility who may need assistance with activities of daily living.
